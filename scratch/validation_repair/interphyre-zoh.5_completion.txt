MECHANISM DISCOVERED
The spec B1 lateral placement (red ball beside green ball at same height) fails completely —
gets 0/30 valid seeds. Empirical investigation revealed the working mechanism:

  OVERLAP-AT-JUNCTION: place red ball at x=green_ball.x, y=holder_y+0.1 (where
  holder_y = green_ball.y - green_ball.radius is the V-junction vertex). This overlaps
  with the green ball. Box2D resolves the overlap via upward impulse, launching the
  green ball out of the V cradle and onto the purple floor.

Key findings:
  1. High-drop (y near 4.5) on right holder bar: achieves ~23% seed exhaustion with
     oracle_steps=500, ~18% with 1000 steps. Many solvable seeds need >500 steps.
  2. Overlap-at-junction at (gb.x, holder_y+0.1): 0% seed exhaustion across 100 seeds
     × 10 variants. Works for ANY red ball radius (even tiny 0.30). First attempt always
     succeeds for ~99.7% of individual (seed, variant) pairs.
  3. Placing at exactly gb.y (green ball center) is degenerate — 63.7% failure rate.
     Must place at holder_y + 0.1 (just above junction), not at center.
