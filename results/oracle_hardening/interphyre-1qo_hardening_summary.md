**Key observations**:

- **just_a_nudge** and **the_cradle** are 100% impossible — confirmed by impossibility
  certificates. Both are flagged for level redesign in the subsequent study.
- **catapult** is 80% impossible in seeds 0–99. The O4 dense-grid audit confirmed this is
  genuine geometric impossibility (ledge height / arm length constraint), not oracle failure.
  The 20% valid seeds are legitimate; the 80% impossible are not recoverable by oracle change.
- **flagpole_sitta** shows 59 valid and 41 exhausted (no trivial). The O3 re-audit found
  46/100 seeds falsely classified as "valid" in the old bundle. The new bundle correctly
  classifies these: some seeds are valid at alternate variants (contributing to 59 valid),
  while 41 seeds have all 10 variants either trivial or impossible (exhausted). The previous
  100% valid report was an artifact of the pre-A4 trivial check.
- **mind_the_gap** shows 30 valid in seeds 0–99, consistent with the O5 finding of 24.6%
  over seeds 0–999 (governing constraint: `platform_y ≤ -3.05`).
- **pinball_machine** shows 11% impossible in seeds 0–99, consistent with the O6 finding
  of 9.3% over seeds 0–999 — below the 10% redesign threshold.
- Mean variant > 2 for several levels (basket_case 2.55, catapult 4.30, falling_into_place
  3.02, flagpole_sitta 3.93, mind_the_gap 4.03, pinball_machine 2.69): these oracles rely
  substantially on geometry fallbacks. This is informational — no threshold was set — but
  suggests the oracles for these levels find fewer valid seeds at variant=0.
